Welcome to Sudoku Adventure!

  • Sudoku is a 9x9 cell puzzle divided into rows, columns and blocks
  • Which means, each sudoku puzzle has 9 rows, 9 columns and 9 blocks
  • You must fill each cell following these constraints:
    1. Every row has a unique number between 1 and 9 inclusive without duplications
    2. Every column has a unique number between 1 and 9 inclusive without duplications
    3. Every block has a unique number between 1 and 9 inclusive without duplications

Good luck and have fun playing!
